Can I see the trophy display and the white Ludens hallway when I visit the office?

Unfortunately, this area is not open to the public as it is the entrance to the private office. In addition, access to the office floor itself is prohibited as a result of building security measures.

Do you accept pitches for new ideas and game concepts?

KOJIMA PRODUCTIONS is focused on internally developed original concepts, and therefore does not accept pitches from external sources.

Can I receive a signature from Hideo Kojima and / or other Kojima Productions staff?

Due to the large volume of similar inquiries it is the policy of KOJIMA PRODUCTIONS to decline all signature requests at this time.

Where can I send presents and letters to KOJIMA PRODUCTIONS?

Thank you for always supporting us, but we do not have a location that accepts letters and presents. Please do note that we cannot reply back to all that letters that are sent to us. Thank you again for your support for KOJIMA PRODUCTIONS.

Where can I purchase KOJIMA PRODUCTIONS merchandise?

KOJIMA PRODUCTIONS limited edition merchandise is currently available at the Shinagawa Season Terrace and Akihabaro UDX Famima!! stores. Official merchandise is also available via the Store section on our website.

Is there an intern program?

There is no intern program at this time. However, a future program is under consideration. Updates will be posted via the Careers page.
